/02 — Goals + Definition of Success

WHAT WINNING
LOOKS LIKE.

Vendor approval is the primary goal. Every deliverable is designed to reduce friction with property management procurement teams.

Primary Goal
ℹ Build a vendor-ready brand and web footprint that supports approval and accelerates trust with property management decision-makers.
Definition of Success — Within 45 Days
  • A professional one-page website with clear multifamily positioning, services, warranties, and conversion CTAs (call + request a bid).
  • A consistent logo and brand baseline usable across proposals, invoices, vendor packets, uniforms, signage, and digital profiles.
  • A credibility-first "vendor-ready" presentation: license number, service area, warranties, and process standards clearly documented.
  • A complete and policy-aligned Google Business Profile to increase local discovery (GBP).
What Vendor Teams Typically Look For
Identity + Contact
Clear company identity with direct contact details, owner/operator name, and service area confirmed on the website.
License + Compliance
Proof of licensing. CA Contractor License #1132904 displayed with a CSLB verify link. W-9, COI availability listed.
Professional Docs
Clean proposals, predictable communication, and organized jobsite standards. Collateral that matches online presence.
Scheduling + Coordination
Responsive scheduling, tenant/resident coordination process documented. Reduces risk perception for property managers.
⚠ We position VPS to meet these expectations without over-claiming. All trust signals are client-provided and verifiable.

/09 — Risk + Expectation Management

WHAT WE WON'T
PROMISE.

We run an honest engagement. The following items are outside our control or require reasonable expectation-setting before work begins.

⚠ No SEO rankings or lead volume guarantees. Baseline SEO is foundational best practice. Ongoing SEO is iterative and results vary by market, competition, and content quality.
⚠ No vendor approval guarantees. We improve credibility signals and reduce procurement friction — but each property management group's approval requirements differ.
⚠ No spam tactics for outreach. Sales Outreach OS is relationship-based. Outcomes depend on list quality, offer clarity, responsiveness, and market conditions.
Known Constraints + Mitigations
No Before/After Photos We emphasize process clarity, warranties, and documentation readiness instead. Credibility without imagery.
GBP Verification Timing GBP verification timeline is controlled by Google. We support all steps, but cannot guarantee completion dates.
Search Display Outcomes Google emphasizes helpful, user-first content. We follow guidance but do not control search display or ranking outcomes.
Client Review Delays Delays in approval turnaround will shift estimated delivery dates proportionally. 2-business-day response windows apply.
